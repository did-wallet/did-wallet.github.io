Your share of expenses

It’s important to accurately capture your share (just the amount you pay if you split expenses with others) of expenses. Remember to include expenses paid for using buy now, pay later services (like StepPay or Afterpay) in the relevant categories. You don’t need to include payments for rent, credit cards, loans, or debt repayments.

Include food, clothing, personal items, transport, health, medical and fitness, children, tertiary education, pets, utilities, communication, entertainment, and domestic travel.

Include expenses for secondary property, private education, personal insurances, counselling, overseas travel, recreational vehicle expenses, and insurances. You don’t need to include items already listed under living expenses.
